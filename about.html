About Us

Our Story

A Kingdom Deserves More Than a Checklist

Your Guide was founded in 2009 by Dorji Wangchuk, a licensed guide who had spent a decade leading international travellers through Bhutan's most remote corners. He noticed the same thing on every trip: visitors left having seen Bhutan, but not having felt it.

So he built something different — a company where every itinerary is designed by someone who grew up here. Where the monastery visit includes a conversation with the monk, not just a photo. Where the trek ends at a farmhouse table, not a hotel lobby. Where Bhutan is experienced, not just ticked off.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

Local First

Every guide, driver, and partner is Bhutanese. Your money stays in the communities you visit.

Depth Over Speed

We design itineraries that slow down. Fewer sites, more meaning. You leave knowing Bhutan, not just having seen it.

Responsible Tourism

We follow Bhutan's high-value, low-impact philosophy. Small groups, minimal footprint, maximum respect for culture and nature.

Honest Expertise

We tell you what's worth your time and what isn't. No filler days, no commission-driven detours.
