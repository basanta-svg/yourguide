Tour Package

Cultural Immersion Tour

7 Days 2–10 people

Dive deep into Bhutan's living culture on this carefully paced 7-day journey. Visit ancient fortresses, masked dance ceremonies, share meals with local families, and explore the country's most iconic dzongs with guides who grew up in their shadows.

Itinerary

Day by Day

Airport welcome, transfer to Thimphu, evening orientation walk.

Overnight in Thimphu

Buddha Dordenma, Tashichho Dzong, weekend market, National Textile Museum.

Overnight in Thimphu

Drive over Dochula Pass, Punakha Dzong, Chimi Lhakhang fertility temple.

Overnight in Punakha

Kyichu Lhakhang, Paro Dzong, National Museum, traditional farmhouse lunch.

Overnight in Paro

Full-day hike to Paro Taktsang monastery, 3,120m elevation.

Overnight in Paro

Authentic village experience, traditional cooking class, local weaving.

Overnight at Village Homestay

Morning at leisure, airport transfer.
