Tour Package

Jomolhari Base Camp Trek

10 Days 2–8 people

Trek beneath Mount Jomolhari through Bhutan's most iconic high-alpine trails. This 10-day expedition climbs through rhododendron forest and yak pastures to Jangothang base camp, crosses the 4,890m Nyile La pass, and descends through Lingshi and Shodu — a true test for experienced trekkers.

Itinerary

Day by Day

Airport welcome in Paro, gear check, trek briefing with your guide, early night before the trail.

Overnight in Paro

Drive to the trailhead, trek roughly 17km alongside the Paro Chhu through pine forest to Shana.

Overnight camp at Shana

A longer day (~22km) climbing through rhododendron forest with the first views of Mount Jomolhari.

Overnight camp at Soi Thangthangka

Trek ~19km to Jangothang, the base camp beneath Mount Jomolhari's dramatic north face.

Overnight camp at Jangothang

Rest and acclimatize with a side hike to the turquoise Tshophu Lakes.

Overnight camp at Jangothang

Cross the 4,890m Nyile La pass, the trek's high point, and descend to the Lingshi valley.

Overnight camp at Lingshi

Follow the Thimphu Chhu downstream through remote alpine scenery to Shodu.

Overnight camp at Shodu

Continue through blue pine and bamboo forest, passing the ruins of Barshong Dzong.

Overnight camp at Barshong

A shorter descent to your final camp, with a farewell dinner shared with your trek crew.

Overnight camp at Dolam Kencho

Final descent to the road head, drive back to Paro, airport transfer.
